How long do LAASH lashes last?+
With proper application and LAASH Sealer, your lashes will last up to 10 days. Avoid oil-based products around the eye area to maximise wear time.
Are they safe for sensitive eyes?+
Yes. The LAASH Bond and Sealer are ophthalmologist-tested and formulated to be gentle. A patch test is recommended for known sensitivities.
How many uses per cluster?+
Each lash cluster can be removed, cleaned, and reapplied up to 10 times. Your kit's 60 clusters give you multiple full sets over time.
Can I shower and swim with them on?+
Yes! Once sealed with LAASH Sealer, your lashes are water-resistant for everyday swimming and showering. Avoid prolonged immersion.

The Method

Four steps. Ten minutes.
Salon-level results.

01
Prep
Clean & Prime

Start with clean, dry lashes free from all mascara and oil-based products. The cleaner the base, the longer the wear.

02
Bond
Apply Bond

Apply LAASH Bond along your natural lash line using the precision applicator. Work in small sections from outer corner inward.

03
Place
Press Clusters

Gently press each LAASH cluster into the bond while it's tacky. Blend them with your natural lashes for a seamless look.

04
Seal
Lock In Place

Apply the LAASH Sealer over all clusters to lock them for up to 10 days. You're done — no salon required.
